A letter from my heart

My Love,

I want to start by saying I am genuinely sorry. I know that many times I get fed up instead of slowing down and trying to truly understand you. I know that this hurts you, and that realization hurts me too. You deserve patience, care, and someone who listens to your heart with softness. I am sorry for the moments when I failed to be that person.

Please believe me when I say that my love for you is real. Even when I make mistakes, even when I say the wrong things, and even when I fail to understand you properly — my heart still chooses you. Always. You are special to me in a way that no one else is. Your presence brings warmth into my life, and your love means more to me than I often manage to express.

I know love is not only about saying “I love you.” It is also about understanding, patience, effort, and emotional safety. I know I need to do better in those parts. I do not want to keep repeating the same mistakes. I want to grow, to listen more carefully, to understand your feelings more deeply, and to love you in the way you truly deserve.

I am sorry for every time I made you feel alone in your emotions. I am sorry for every time my frustration became bigger than my understanding. You have a beautiful heart, and I never want to be the reason it feels heavy. If I could take away the hurt I caused, I would. Since I cannot undo it, all I can do is be honest, hold myself accountable, and try with my whole heart to become better for us.

I do not want perfection between us. I want truth, healing, and love that becomes stronger after hard days. I want to create a relationship where you feel safe, cherished, heard, and deeply loved. I want to be someone who brings you peace, not pain.

Thank you for loving me, even in my imperfect moments. Thank you for being you. And thank you for being someone I care about so deeply. I am sorry, truly. And I hope this apology reaches your heart with the sincerity with which it was written.

No matter what, I love you. And I will keep trying to show that love with more understanding, more patience, and more care.

Forever yours. 💗
